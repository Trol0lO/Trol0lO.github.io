Our Shelters

“We started from nothing… zero knowledge on how a shelter should be run.
But seeing the growth and happiness of the rescues, we really would not have it any other way.”


The Pawssion Project Shelters in Bacolod City and SJDM, Bulacan currently houses a total of 400 rescues dogs and 100 rescued cats combined. Our rescues are free-roaming, fully vaccinated, spayed & neutered and fed 2 times a day with snacks and vitamins! We are also employing 12 stay-in Caretakers, our resident Vet, Dr. Anjuli Garingalao, and have started building a small clinic in our Bulacan Shelter.

Our Shelters are open for Visitors and Volunteers every Monday to Sunday, 10AM to 5PM. BOOK YOUR VISIT NOW!


PLEASE BRING YOUR VACCINATION CARD AND WEAR YOUR MASK AT ALL TIMES. NO CARD, NO ENTRY. MAXIMUM OF 15 PEOPLE AT A TIME.
