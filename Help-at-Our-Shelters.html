Help at Our Shelters

If you can’t Adopt, Foster or Sponsor, there are always other ways to help!

Visitors are always welcome to drop by our Shelters in Bacolod and Bulacan,  from 10AM to 5PM, 7 Days a Week!

Volunteers who choose to spend time with our rescues play an important role in their rehabilitation and socialization. Most of the dogs at our shelters love to walk, play and get some quality time out of the environment they inhabit on a daily basis. Enrichment for the animals is one of the key factors to their wellbeing, so just getting more people to spend time with them and help supervise them, will already do wonders! 

NOTE! Although all our rescues are up-to-date with their vaccinations, we still advice our visitors to make sure that their Tetanus Vaccinations are up to date. You can also bring your own pets along, but would have to present proof that their vaccinations are up-to-date as well .
